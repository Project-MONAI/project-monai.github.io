Medical Open Network for AI

About MONAI

Project MONAI was originally initiated by NVIDIA & King’s College London to establish an inclusive community of AI researchers for the development and exchange of best practices for AI in healthcare imaging across academia and enterprise researchers. The MONAI platform is the open-source foundation being created by Project MONAI and it wouldn’t have been possible to accelerate this development without the development of existing toolkits like Nvidia Clara Train, NiftyNet, DeepNeuro, DLTK.

MONAI marks the bringing together of these existing efforts to build a common, open foundation and it is mission-critical for MONAI’s success to be guided by thought leaders in the domain.
