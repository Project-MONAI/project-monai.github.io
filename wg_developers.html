Developers Committee

Mission Statement

The Developers Committee Working Group serves as the central coordination point for MONAI Core development, ensuring engineering excellence, maintaining code quality, and driving technical decisions. This committee oversees the architectural evolution of MONAI, establishes development standards, and coordinates engineering efforts across the project.
